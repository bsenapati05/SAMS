About College

Established in 1959, Dhenkanal Autonomous College stands as a premier seat of higher learning in Odisha, committed to the pursuit of academic excellence and holistic student development. Recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) under sections 2(f) and 12(B) and affiliated with Utkal University, the institution enjoys Autonomous status, which empowers it to curate industry-aligned curricula and maintain a rigorous, independent examination system. The college offers a diverse academic portfolio across the Arts, Science, and Commerce streams, including specialized Undergraduate (Honours) and Postgraduate programs designed to foster intellectual growth and research aptitude. With a sprawling, eco-friendly campus, a well-stocked digital library, and modern laboratory facilities, the college provides a conducive environment for both academic and extracurricular pursuits through units like the NCC, NSS, and various career counseling initiatives. Accredited by NAAC, Dhenkanal Autonomous College continues to uphold its legacy of transforming students into socially responsible, skilled, and ethically sound global citizens.
